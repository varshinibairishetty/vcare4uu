Mental Health Tips

  • Practice mindfulness and meditation.
  • Stay physically active; exercise can boost your mood.
  • Connect with friends and family to share your thoughts and feelings.
  • Seek professional help if you're feeling overwhelmed.
  • Establish a regular sleep schedule for better rest.
